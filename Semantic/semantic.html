5 Skills Not Yet Possessed by Artificial Intelligence (AI) That Are Essential to Master in the Digital Age

Currently, Artificial Intelligence (AI) is going viral due to the emergence of new technologies developed by OpenAI. OpenAI is an artificial intelligence research laboratory founded by Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, and others in 2015. They develop and promote AI that is friendly and beneficial to human life.

However, behind the many benefits of AI, it has displaced many blue-collar workers whose jobs involve physical labor, replacing them with technology. The displacement of blue-collar workers can also disrupt white-collar jobs, such as copywriters, content writers, beginner programmers, and other knowledge workers. Therefore, the development of the following soft skills is needed to play a significant role in facing technological advancements in the future.

Here are the five essential skills:

Creativity

The first skill that cannot be replaced by technology is creativity. This skill involves generating new ideas, making interesting discoveries, and creating art. While there are AI systems that can assist with creative tasks, the true essence of creativity, including artistic expression, is uniquely human.

Innovation

Innovation is the ability to think critically and come up with new solutions or approaches to problems. It involves a mindset that continuously seeks improvement and the development of new ideas, products, processes, services, and more. While technology can support innovation, it is the human capacity to identify and address real-world challenges that drives true innovation.

Problem Solving

Problem-solving is the process of finding effective solutions to complex issues. This skill requires listening, analyzing, researching, collaborating, and making decisions based on context and understanding. While AI can assist with data analysis, it lacks the human capacity for nuanced problem-solving and decision-making.

Communication

Communication is a skill that involves conveying ideas, thoughts, and emotions effectively. It requires understanding context, adapting to various scenarios, and often dealing with complex human emotions and interpersonal dynamics. AI lacks the emotional intelligence and adaptability required for nuanced human communication.

Interpersonal Relationships

Interpersonal relationships involve building and maintaining personal connections within communities and groups. These relationships are based on trust, empathy, and shared experiences. They require complex emotional and social understanding, which AI lacks due to its absence of human emotions and experiences.

Therefore, as humans, we should focus on honing these five skills because they are not easily replaceable by artificial intelligence and are critical in navigating the upcoming technological revolution.
